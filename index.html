MY03 is a free call forwarding service brought to you by WHYPAY?, the UK conference call company which is saving millions of pound every month for its users.

To use MY03, you simply need to provide a landline destination number for your calls and we'll give you a dedicated 03 number to share with colleagues.

▼ Privacy
Sometimes we’d prefer to keep our home telephone number private, sharing it only with our family and friends. MY03 allows you to do so by providing you with an 03 number which simply redirects calls instantly to your home telephone number.

▼ Permanence
If you move house, simply contact us [mailto:helpdesk@whypay.net?subject=MY03 - Please update my destination number&body=My existing home phone number is: (enter number here)] letting us know your existing home number and we’ll update your 03 number to route calls to your new home number. This allows you to keep a permanent telephone number for people to reach you on.

▼ No additional cost
We make no charge for providing this service and calls to your 03 number will be charged in exactly the same ways as calls to your existing 01 or 02 number.

▼ Security
Using an 03 number to protect your privacy and ensure that your home number isn’t divulged to third parties, is a great way of ensuring you can work from home effectively. However, what about the security of the call when using the MY03 number translation service? Here at WHYPAY?, as a Communications Provider, we ensure that as soon as a caller dials your 03 number, your call is routed through our secure infrastructure to your 03 number, and a simple call diversion is implemented that routes the call directly to your landline number. Calls are not intercepted or manipulated in any other way, and you can be assured that your calls are as secure as making a normal telephone call. For added peace of mind, WHYPAY?’s switching equipment and interconnections are located in secure industry leading UK data centres with stringent physical and remote access controls, and our procedures comply with the latest GDPR regulations with respect to privacy and data security.
